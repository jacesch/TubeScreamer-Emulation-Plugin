# TubeScreamer-Emulation-Plugin
ScreamerTube is a faithful digital emulation of the classic Tube Screamer overdrive pedal, designed for musicians, producers, and audio engineers who want the iconic mid-boosted overdrive sound directly in their DAW.

**Features**
Classic Tube Screamer Tone – Emulates the warm, smooth overdrive of the original TS-style pedal.
Drive, Tone, and Level Controls – Shape your overdrive from subtle warmth to aggressive crunch.
ADAA (Anti-Derivative Anti-Aliasing) – Reduces digital aliasing artifacts for a smoother, more natural overdrive sound, especially at high gain.
Stereo Support – Works with both mono and stereo signals.
Low CPU Usage – Optimized for real-time performance in any DAW.
VST3 & AU Support – Compatible with most major DAWs on macOS.

**Installation**
Copy the ScreamerTube.vst3 or ScreamerTube.component file into your DAW's plugin directory:
VST3: ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/
AU: ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/
Restart your DAW and scan for new plugins.
Insert ScreamerTube on a track and start playing!

**Usage**
Drive – Controls the amount of overdrive applied to the signal.
Tone – Adjusts the tonal character; roll off highs for smoother sound or brighten for more bite.
Level – Sets the output volume to match your DAW track level.
ADAA – Anti-Derivative Anti-Aliasing reduces aliasing artifacts, ensuring smoother overdrive, especially at high gain settings.

Experiment with stacking multiple instances or combining with other effects for unique tones!

**Development / Source**
This plugin was developed in MATLAB Audio Toolbox using digital signal processing techniques to emulate the Tube Screamer circuit while implementing ADAA for high-quality digital overdrive.
